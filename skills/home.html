<h2>Skills</h2>

<p><em>For the pre-release planning manual, see <a href="/manual:Skills_(2000)">Prerelease Skills plan</a>.</em></p>

 

<p><strong>Skills</strong> are levels of abilities for a <a href="/player">player</a> to perform a given task. Skills can increase a player's ability in combat, gathering resources, processing materials, or performing other tasks.</p>

 

<h3>List of skills</h3>

<p>There are 18 skills in <em>RuneScape Classic</em>:</p>

<p>Each skill has a maximum of 536,870,911 experience points before experience rolls over. Reaching level 99 in a skill requires 13,034,431 experience points. Combat-related skills (Attack, Strength, Defense, Hits, Prayer, Ranged, Magic) also affect your <a href="/Combat_level">Combat level</a>.</p>

<h3>Trivia</h3>

<ul>

 <li><strong><a href="/Tailoring">Tailoring</a></strong> was never completed and later merged into <a href="/Crafting">Crafting</a>.</li>

 <li><a href="/Influence">Influence</a> was a planned skill but was replaced by <a href="/Quest_points">Quest points</a>.</li>

 <li><a href="/Carpentry">Carpentry</a> was planned for player housing and later became <a href="/rsw:Construction">Construction</a> in RuneScape 2.</li>

 <li><strong>Summoning spells</strong> were proposed under <a href="/Magic">Magic</a> but later released as a full <a href="/rsw:Summoning">skill</a> in RuneScape 2.</li>

 <li>A <strong>hiding</strong> stat once existed but had no supporting gear.</li>

 <li>No player maxed <strong>Prayer</strong> in RuneScape Classic. <strong>Lilyuffie88</strong> was the first to pass 10M XP in it.</li>

</ul>
