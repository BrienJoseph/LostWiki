Skills

For the pre-release planning manual, see Prerelease Skills plan.

Skills are levels of abilities for a player to perform a given task. Skills can increase a player's ability in combat, gathering resources, processing materials, or performing other tasks.

List of skills

There are 18 skills in RuneScape Classic:

SkillAbility
AttackInflict damage more often in combat; unlocks better weapons.
DefenseBlock attacks more often; unlocks better armour.
StrengthInflict higher damage in combat.
HitsAmount of damage a player can endure before dying.
RangedHit enemies with projectiles like bows and arrows.
PrayerUse prayers to enhance other skills or defend in combat.
MagicCast spells using runes for damage, utility, or teleportation.
CookingCook food to restore Hits.
WoodcuttingUse an axe to cut trees for logs.
FletchingMake bows, arrows, and projectile weapons.
FishingCatch raw fish from fishing spots.
FiremakingLight logs using a tinderbox.
CraftingMake pottery, jewellery, glassware, etc.
SmithingSmelt ores and smith metal bars into gear.
MiningMine ores from rocks with a pickaxe.
HerblawMix herbs into potions that boost skills.
AgilityNavigate shortcuts.
ThievingPickpocket NPCs and steal from stalls.

Each skill has a maximum of 536,870,911 experience points before experience rolls over. Reaching level 99 in a skill requires 13,034,431 experience points. Combat-related skills (Attack, Strength, Defense, Hits, Prayer, Ranged, Magic) also affect your Combat level.

Trivia

  • Tailoring was never completed and later merged into Crafting.
  • Influence was a planned skill but was replaced by Quest points.
  • Carpentry was planned for player housing and later became Construction in RuneScape 2.
  • Summoning spells were proposed under Magic but later released as a full skill in RuneScape 2.
  • A hiding stat once existed but had no supporting gear.
  • No player maxed Prayer in RuneScape Classic. Lilyuffie88 was the first to pass 10M XP in it.
