Attack is a melee combat skill in RuneScape Classic that determines the accuracy of the player's melee attacks. Having a higher Attack level allows players to hit fewer zeroes against opponents with high Defense or armour.

In both cases, H = the amount of Hits experience received from the kill. This is different for each opponent, and unlike in RuneScape 2, is unrelated to the opponents' Hits.

Weapons

As players gain Attack levels, they are able to use better weapons. Better weapons have higher WeaponAim and WeaponPower bonuses, meaning that players are not only able to hit fewer zeroes but can also inflict more damage.
