Doric's Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Any Pickaxe

Starting Location: Doric's Anvil (North of Falador)

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • 400 Mining Experience
  • 180 Gold Coins
  • Permission to use Doric's Anvils

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Doric (North-West of Falador) and accept his quest to gather ores.

Getting a Pickaxe (Optional)

  • If you don't have a pickaxe, go to Barbarian Village and pick up a bronze pickaxe from one of the buildings.

Mining the Required Ores

  • Go to the Dwarven Mines (North-East of Falador) and climb down the trapdoor.
  • Mine 2 Iron Ore.
  • Mine 4 Copper Ore.
  • Mine 6 Clay.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to Doric with 6 Clay, 4 Copper, and 2 Iron.
  • Give him the ores to complete the quest and receive your reward.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ 400 Mining Experience
  • ✅ 180 Gold Coins
  • ✅ Permission to use Doric's Anvils
