Imp Catcher Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements: None

Starting Location: 3rd Floor of Wizard's Tower, South of Draynor Village

Reward:

  • 375 Magic Experience
  • 1 Quest Point
  • Amulet of Accuracy (+4 Weapon Aim)

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Wizard Mizgog on the 3rd floor of Wizard's Tower.
  • Accept the quest to retrieve the 4 missing beads: Red, White, Black, and Yellow.

Collecting the Beads

  • Kill Imps found around the Wizard's Tower and other locations to collect the beads.
  • Alternatively, buy the beads from other players to save time.
  • Collect all 4 beads: Red, White, Black, and Yellow.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to Wizard Mizgog with all 4 beads.
  • Talk to him to complete the quest and claim your reward.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 375 Magic Experience
  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ Amulet of Accuracy (+4 Weapon Aim)
