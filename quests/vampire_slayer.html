Vampire Slayer Quest Guide

Difficulty: Medium

Requirements:

  • Hammer
  • Decent armor and weapon
  • Few coins to buy beer

Starting Location: Morgan's House in Draynor Village

Reward:

  • 3 Quest Points
  • Attack & Defence Experience

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Morgan in Draynor Village.
  • He will tell you about Count Draynor and suggest you speak with Dr. Harlow in Varrock.

Preparing to Fight the Vampire

  • Before leaving, go upstairs in Morgan's house and search the cupboard to obtain Garlic.
  • Head to the Jolly Boar Inn (northeast of Varrock, near the Wilderness ditch).
  • Speak to Dr. Harlow and buy him a beer when he asks for one.
  • He will give you a Stake and explain how to kill the vampire.

Fighting Count Draynor

  • Prepare for battle (armor, weapon, garlic, stake, and hammer).
  • Travel to Draynor Manor (north of Draynor Village).
  • Enter the manor and go down to the basement via the stairs.
  • Open the coffin to summon Count Draynor (level 34).
  • Fight the vampire — once his health is low, you will automatically stake him (if you have the stake and hammer in your inventory).

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 3 Quest Points
  • ✅ Attack & Defence Experience
