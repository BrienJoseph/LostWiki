Rune Mysteries Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • None.

Starting Location: Duke Horacio on the 2nd floor of Lumbridge Castle

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • Ability to mine Rune Essence
  • Free teleport to Rune Essence mine via Aubury (Varrock) or Sedridor (Wizard's Tower)

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle and receive the Air Talisman.
  • Deliver the Air Talisman to Sedridor in the Wizard's Tower basement.

Delivering the Research

  • Receive research notes from Sedridor.
  • Take the notes to Aubury at the rune shop in Varrock (east of Varrock Square).
  • Return to Sedridor with Aubury's findings.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ Access to Rune Essence mining
  • ✅ Free Rune Essence teleport from Aubury or Sedridor
