Pirate's Treasure Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • 60 Coins
  • White Apron
  • Spade

Starting Location: The Pub in Port Sarim

Reward:

  • 2 Quest Points
  • 450 Coins
  • Cut Emerald
  • Gold Ring

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Redbeard Frank in the Port Sarim Pub and agree to bring him Karamja Rum.

Smuggling the Rum

  • Travel to Karamja and buy Karamja Rum (30 Coins) from the Wine Shop.
  • Talk to Luthas (northeast of the shop) and agree to help him with bananas.
  • Pick 10 Bananas from the field west of Luthas's house.
  • Fill the crate with bananas, place the Rum in the crate, and finish filling it with bananas.
  • Talk to Luthas for 30 Coins.

Recovering the Rum

  • Return to Port Sarim.
  • Wear a White Apron. (Buy from Varrock Clothing Shop if needed.)
  • Talk to Wydin at the Food Store west of the dock to get hired.
  • Search the crates inside the store to find and retrieve the Rum.

Finding the Treasure Location

  • Return to Redbeard Frank and give him the Rum.
  • Receive the key from Frank.
  • Go to the Blue Moon Inn in Varrock and use the key on the chest upstairs (west room).
