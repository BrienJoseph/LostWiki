Black Knights' Fortress Quest Guide

Difficulty: Medium

Requirements:

  • 12 Quest Points
  • Bronze Medium Helmet
  • Iron Chainbody

Starting Location: 2nd Floor of White Knights' Castle, Falador

Reward: 2500 Coins, 3 Quest Points

Steps

Preparation

  • Obtain a Bronze Medium Helmet
  • Obtain an Iron Chainbody
  • Have at least 12 Quest Points

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Sir Amik Varze in the White Knights' Castle and accept the quest

Getting a Cabbage

  • Go west of Ice Mountain to the Monastery
  • Pick a cabbage from the patch north of the Monastery

Infiltrating the Black Knights' Fortress

  • Equip Bronze Medium Helmet & Iron Chainbody
  • Enter the fortress through the southeast door
  • Push the wall and climb up 2 ladders
  • Climb down the southeast ladder and go east through two doors
  • Climb up the ladder near the cannons
  • Climb down the east ladder to the Zamorak altar room
  • Open the door, handle the Black Knight, and climb the southwest ladder

Spying on the Black Knights

  • Listen at the Grill to learn about the secret weapon and the cabbage weakness

Sabotaging the Weapon

  • Go back, push the wall, and enter the eastern door
  • Climb the ladder after dealing with the guards/Black Knights
  • Head east, then south, and push the wall to reveal a hole
  • Use your cabbage on the hole to destroy the weapon

Finishing the Quest

  • Return to Sir Amik Varze for your reward

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 2500 Coins
  • ✅ 3 Quest Points
