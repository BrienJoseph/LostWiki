Witch's Potion Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • 300 Coins (to buy an Eye of Newt)

Starting Location: Hetty the Witch in Rimmington

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • 275 Magic Experience

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Hetty in Rimmington and agree to help her brew a potion.
  • She will ask you to gather four ingredients: Onion, Burnt Meat, Eye of Newt, and Rat's Tail.

Collecting the Ingredients

Onion

  • Found in the field northwest of Rimmington. Pick one.

Burnt Meat

  • Kill a giant rat east of Rimmington and collect raw rat meat.
  • Cook the meat on the range in the house just north of Hetty’s house.
  • If successfully cooked, cook it again to burn it (you need burnt meat).

Rat's Tail

  • Kill a giant rat (only after starting the quest) to obtain a rat's tail.

Eye of Newt

  • Purchase an Eye of Newt from the Magic Shop in Port Sarim (costs 300 coins).

Completing the Potion

  • Return to Hetty in Rimmington with all 4 ingredients.
  • Speak to her, then click on the cauldron to drink the potion.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ 275 Magic Experience
