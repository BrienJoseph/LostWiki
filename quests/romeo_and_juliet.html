Romeo & Juliet Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Cadaver Berries (found east of the Stone Circle south of Varrock)

Starting Location: Juliet's House, west of Varrock on the north side of the road

Reward:

  • 5 Quest Points

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Juliet on her balcony and agree to deliver a message to Romeo.
  • Find Romeo in Varrock Square and give him Juliet's message.

Getting Help from Father Lawrence

  • Go to Father Lawrence in the chapel northeast of Varrock.
  • Learn about the Cadaver Potion plan and the need to visit the Apothecary.

Getting the Cadaver Potion

  • Go to the Apothecary (southwest of Varrock Square).
  • Talk to the Apothecary twice (with Cadaver Berries in your inventory) to receive the Cadaver Potion.

Finishing the Plan

  • Take the Cadaver Potion to Juliet.
  • Return to Romeo and tell him Juliet has the potion.
  • Watch Romeo misunderstand and think Juliet has died.
