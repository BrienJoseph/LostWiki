Sheep Shearer Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Shears (buy from a general store for 1 coin)

Starting Location: Farmer Fred's house, just west of the sheep pen, northwest of Lumbridge

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • 200 Crafting XP
  • 60 Coins

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Farmer Fred (northwest of Lumbridge near the sheep pen) and accept the quest.

Getting the Shears

  • Go south to the Lumbridge General Store.
  • Buy shears for 1 coin.

Shearing Sheep & Spinning Wool

  • Shear 20 sheep in Fred's pen to collect 20 wool.
  • Go to the 2nd floor of Lumbridge Castle.
  • Use the spinning wheel in the middle room to spin 20 balls of wool.

Finishing the Quest

  • Return to Farmer Fred with 20 balls of wool.
  • Receive your reward.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ 200 Crafting XP
  • ✅ 60 Coins
