Shield of Arrav Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy - Medium

Requirements:

  • 20-40 Coins
  • A friend who has not completed this quest (required)

Starting Location: Reldo in the Library of Varrock Palace

Reward:

  • 2 Half-Certificates (combine with your friend's to complete)
  • 600 Coins (shared reward)
  • 1 Quest Point

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Reldo in the Varrock Palace Library and ask for a quest.
  • Find and read the book about the Shield of Arrav on the bookshelf nearby.
  • Return to Reldo and he will tell you to speak to Baraek in the Varrock marketplace.

Learning About the Gangs

  • Speak to Baraek (fur trader in Varrock Square) and pay him 20 coins for gang information.
  • Go to the alley behind the Sword Shop and speak to the Tramp about the Black Arm Gang.

Choosing Your Gang

Black Arm Gang Path

  • Speak to Katrine in the Black Arm Gang hideout (southwest Varrock) and ask to join.
  • Katrine will task you with stealing 2 Phoenix Crossbows from the Phoenix Gang's weapon storage.
  • Have your friend join the Phoenix Gang.
  • Your friend must kill Jonny the Beard (level 2) in the Blue Moon Inn and obtain the Intelligence Report.
  • Your friend gives the report to the Phoenix Gang leader to receive the Weapon Store Key.
  • Your friend retrieves 2 Phoenix Crossbows and the other half of the Shield from the Phoenix Gang base.
  • Your friend trades the crossbows to you.
  • Return to Katrine with the crossbows to gain access to the Black Arm Gang's shield room.
  • Retrieve your half of the Shield of Arrav.

Phoenix Gang Path

  • Speak to Baraek for gang information (20 coins).
  • Kill Jonny the Beard (level 2) in the Blue Moon Inn and take the Intelligence Report.
  • Go to the Phoenix Gang hideout (marked with a dungeon symbol, east of Varrock's south wall).
  • Give the Intelligence Report to Straven, the Phoenix Gang leader.
  • Receive the Weapon Store Key.
  • Enter the Phoenix Weapon Store and collect the 2 Phoenix Crossbows (for your Black Arm Gang friend).
  • Collect your half of the Shield of Arrav from the Phoenix Gang base.

Completing the Quest

  • Both players take their shield halves to Curator Haig Halen at the Varrock Museum.
  • He will give each player a certificate half.
  • Trade your certificate half with your friend to complete the full certificate.
  • Take the complete certificate to King Roald in Varrock Palace to claim your reward.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ 600 Coins (split between you and your friend)
