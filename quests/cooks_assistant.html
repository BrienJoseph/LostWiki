Cook's Assistant Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Flour

Starting Location: Lumbridge Castle

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • 300 Cooking Experience
  • Permission to use the Cook's range

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to the Cook in Lumbridge Castle and offer to help him get ingredients for the cake.

Gathering the Ingredients

Flour

  • Get a pot from Lumbridge Castle.
  • Go northwest to the windmill.
  • Pick a grain from the field west of the windmill.
  • Go to the top floor of the windmill and use grain on the hopper.
  • Operate the hopper controls.
  • Go back down and collect the flour in your pot.

Egg

  • Go northeast of Lumbridge to the chicken farm.
  • Pick up an egg near the chickens.

Milk

  • Enter the nearby house in the farm and get a bucket.
  • Go east to the cows.
  • Use the bucket on a dairy cow to get milk.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to the Cook in Lumbridge Castle and give him the ingredients to complete the quest.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ 300 Cooking Experience
  • ✅ Permission to use the Cook's range
