Demon Slayer Quest Guide

Difficulty: Medium

Requirements:

  • 25 Regular Bones
  • 1 Coin

Starting Location: Gypsy's Tent in Varrock Square

Reward:

  • 3 Quest Points
  • Silverlight Sword (useful for fighting demons)

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to the Gypsy in Varrock Square and pay her 1 coin to learn about Delrith.
  • Ask her for the magical incantation.

Getting Silverlight

  • Talk to Sir Prysin in the southwest room of Varrock Castle about Silverlight.
  • Go upstairs and talk to Captain Rovin for the first key.
  • Go to the northeast room upstairs in the castle and grab a bucket.
  • Fill the bucket with water from the sink downstairs.
  • Use the bucket of water on the drain outside the castle.
  • Enter the Varrock sewers via the manhole and retrieve the second key.
  • Collect 25 regular bones and go to Wizard's Tower.
  • Talk to Wizard Traiborn and give him the bones to receive the third key.
  • Return to Sir Prysin with all 3 keys to receive Silverlight.

Defeating Delrith

  • Head south of Varrock to the Dark Wizards' Stone Circle.
  • Wield Silverlight and fight Delrith until he is weakened.
  • Choose the correct incantation: "Carlem Aber Camerinthum Purchai Gabindo" to banish Delrith.

Completing the Quest

  • Once Delrith is defeated, return to claim your reward!

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 3 Quest Points
  • ✅ Silverlight Sword
