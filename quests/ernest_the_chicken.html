Ernest the Chicken Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements: None

Starting Location: Outside Draynor Manor

Reward:

  • 300 Coins
  • 4 Quest Points

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Veronica outside Draynor Manor and offer to help find Ernest.

Talk to Professor Oddenstein

  • Enter the manor and go to the top floor.
  • Talk to Professor Oddenstein. He needs 3 items to fix his machine: Oil Can, Rubber Tube, and Pressure Gauge.

Getting the Oil Can (Basement Maze)

  • Pull A and B levers. Open the NE door.
  • Pull D lever. Open the SW door, then the South door.
  • Pull A and B levers again. Open the NW door and the West door.
  • Pull F and E levers. Open the East door twice.
  • Pull C lever. Open the NW door and the West door.
  • Pull E lever. Open the East door, then the South door twice.
  • Open the West door and grab the Oil Can.

Getting the Rubber Tube

  • Go to the ground floor east room and pick up the Spade.
  • Go outside to the compost heap (north, west, south of manor).
  • Use the Spade on the compost to get a Key.
  • Return to the manor, use the Key on the door with the Skeleton, and grab the Rubber Tube.

Getting the Pressure Gauge

  • Go to the second floor southeast room and pick up Fish Food.
  • Go to the west room on the ground floor and pick up Poison.
  • Use Poison on Fish Food.
  • Go southwest of the manor to the fountain.
  • Use the Poisoned Fish Food on the fountain and search it to get the Pressure Gauge.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to Professor Oddenstein with all 3 items to change Ernest back into a human.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 300 Coins
  • ✅ 4 Quest Points
