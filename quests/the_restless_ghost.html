The Restless Ghost Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Ability to kill a Level 19 Skeleton (optional — can be avoided)

Starting Location: Father Aereck in Lumbridge Chapel

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • Prayer Experience

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Father Aereck inside Lumbridge Chapel.
  • He will ask you to speak with Father Urhney about banishing a ghost.

Finding Father Urhney

  • Head west of Lumbridge, around the castle, and then south through the swamp.
  • Locate Father Urhney's house in the southeastern corner of the swamp.
  • Speak to Father Urhney to receive the Amulet of Ghostspeak.

Talking to the Ghost

  • Return to Lumbridge Graveyard behind the chapel.
  • Wear the Amulet of Ghostspeak and talk to the ghost in the coffin.
  • The ghost will explain that a warlock stole his skull and took it to the Wizard's Tower.

Recovering the Skull

  • Travel to the Wizard's Tower (south of Draynor Village).
  • Go down the ladder to the basement of the tower.
  • Find the skull on the floor. Picking it up will spawn a Level 19 Skeleton.
  • Kill the skeleton or simply grab the skull and run.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to the Lumbridge Graveyard.
  • Use the skull on the ghost's coffin to free his spirit.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
  • ✅ Prayer Experience
