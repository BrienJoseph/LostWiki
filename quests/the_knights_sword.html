The Knight's Sword Quest Guide

Difficulty: Medium

Requirements:

  • Pickaxe
  • Level 10 Mining
  • Redberry Pie (10 Cooking required to make)
  • 2 Iron Bars (15 Smithing to make yourself)

Starting Location: Courtyard of the White Knights' Castle in Falador

Reward:

  • 1 Quest Point
  • A large amount of Smithing experience (amount scales with your Smithing level)

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Speak to Squire Asrol in the White Knights' Castle courtyard in Falador.
  • He will direct you to speak to Reldo, the librarian at Varrock Castle Library.

Finding the Incando Dwarf

  • Speak to Reldo in Varrock Castle Library about the Incando Dwarves.
  • He will tell you about Thurgo, an Incando Dwarf who lives south of Port Sarim.

Visiting Thurgo

  • Bring a Redberry Pie to Thurgo (southwest of Port Sarim near Mudskipper Point).
  • Give him the pie and ask him about making a sword.
  • Thurgo needs a picture of the sword to create a replica.

Finding the Sword Design

  • Return to the White Knights' Castle in Falador.
  • Go to the 3rd floor (east side) and search the cupboard in Sir Vyvin's room while he is not present to obtain the portrait.

Mining Blurite Ore

  • Return to Thurgo with the portrait.
  • Thurgo will tell you he needs Blurite Ore from the nearby cave.
  • Enter the cave on the cliff above Thurgo's house.
  • Navigate to the back of the cave past Ice Warriors and Ice Giants.
  • Mine at least 1 Blurite Ore (recommended: bring extra if possible).

Finishing the Sword

  • Bring Thurgo the Blurite Ore and 2 Iron Bars.
  • Thurgo will craft the new sword for you.

Completing the Quest

  • Return to Squire Asrol at the White Knights' Castle and give him the sword.
  • Quest Complete!

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 1 Quest Point
