Goblin Diplomacy Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy - Medium

Requirements:

  • 50+ Coins
  • Optional: Weapon for killing goblins

Starting Location: Rusty Anchor Pub, Port Sarim

Reward:

  • 5 Quest Points
  • 815 Crafting Experience
  • 1 Gold Bar

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to the Bartender at the Rusty Anchor in Port Sarim to start the quest.

Go to Goblin Village

  • Walk north through Falador to reach Goblin Village.
  • Talk to General Wartface and General Bentnoze.
  • Kill goblins until you have 3 Goblin Armors.

Gather Dye Ingredients

  • Buy 3 Redberries from the food store in Port Sarim.
  • Pick 2 Onions west of Port Sarim.
  • Go to Falador Park and pay Wyson the Gardener 20gp for 2 Woad Leaves.

Make the Dyes

  • Go to Aggie the Witch in Draynor Village.
  • Have her make Red, Yellow, and Blue Dyes (5gp each).
  • Combine Red Dye and Yellow Dye to make Orange Dye.

Dye the Goblin Armors

  • Use Orange Dye on one Goblin Armor.
  • Use Blue Dye on another Goblin Armor.
  • Leave one Goblin Armor its original color (brown).

Finish the Quest

  • Return to Goblin Village.
  • Give the armors to General Bentnoze in this order: Orange, Blue, Brown.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 5 Quest Points
  • ✅ 815 Crafting Experience
  • ✅ 1 Gold Bar
