Prince Ali Rescue Quest Guide

Difficulty: Easy

Requirements:

  • Rope
  • Wig (4 Balls of Wool)
  • Yellow Dye (2 Onions + 5gp)
  • Red Berries
  • 2 Buckets of Water
  • Flour
  • Ashes
  • Pink Skirt
  • 3 Beers
  • Soft Clay
  • Bronze Bar

Starting Location: Talk to Hassan in Al Kharid Palace

Reward:

  • 3 Quest Points
  • 700 Coins
  • Free access through the Al Kharid gate

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to Hassan in Al Kharid Palace.
  • Talk to Osman for instructions.

Gathering Items and Preparing the Disguise

  • Talk to Leela in Draynor Village for advice and a checklist.
  • Talk to Aggie the Witch for Skin Paste and Yellow Dye.
  • Talk to Ned in Draynor Village to get Rope and Wig made from wool.

Getting the Key Imprint

  • Talk to Lady Keli and compliment her.
  • Ask to see and touch the key while having Soft Clay in your inventory.

Getting the Key

  • Take the Key Imprint and a Bronze Bar to Osman in Al Kharid.
  • Pick up the Key from Leela in Draynor Village.

Rescuing Prince Ali

  • Talk to the Jail Guard near Lady Keli and give him 3 Beers to get him drunk.
  • Tie up Lady Keli using Rope.
  • Use the Key to unlock the prison door.
  • Give the disguise items to Prince Ali and he will escape.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 3 Quest Points
  • ✅ 700 Coins
  • ✅ Free access through Al Kharid Gate
