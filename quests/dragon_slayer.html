Dragon Slayer Quest Guide

Difficulty: Hard

Requirements:

  • 6 Steel Bars or 12 Nails
  • 3 Planks
  • 2,000 gp (or 10,000 gp if you can't telegrab)
  • Unfired Bowl
  • Lobster Pot
  • Silk
  • Wizard Mind Bomb
  • Items to kill Elvarg (Gear, Food, Potions)

Starting Location: Champion's Guild

Reward:

  • 20,000 Strength XP
  • 20,000 Defence XP
  • Ability to wear Rune Platebody (84,500 gp)
  • Ability to wear Green Dragonhide Body (10,400 gp)

Steps

Starting the Quest

  • Talk to the Guildmaster at the Champion's Guild.
  • Go to Oziach near Edgeville for more information and get the key to Melzar's Maze.

Getting the Map Pieces

  • Visit the Oracle on Ice Mountain with Silk, Mind Bomb, Bowl, and Lobster Pot. Go into the Dwarven Mine and search the chest for the first map piece.
  • Go to Melzar's Maze (south of the Crafting Guild) with food and the maze key. Follow the rooms, kill enemies, collect keys, and defeat Melzar and the Lesser Demon for the second map piece.
  • Go to Port Sarim Jail. Kill or pay Wormbrain for the third map piece. Telegrab it if you kill him.
  • Combine all 3 map pieces.

Getting the Dragonfire Shield

  • Talk to Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle to get the Dragonfire Shield.

Buying and Repairing the Ship

  • Gather 2,000 gp, Hammer, 3 Planks, and 12 Nails.
  • Go to Port Sarim and buy the ship from Klarense.
  • Repair the ship using the planks and nails.
  • Talk to Ned in Draynor Village to sail to Crandor.

Fighting Elvarg

  • Sail to Crandor with Ned.
  • Navigate through Crandor avoiding enemies.
  • Enter the cave to find Elvarg.
  • Equip the Dragonfire Shield and kill Elvarg.

Completing the Quest

  • Escape Crandor through the cave exit to Karamja.
  • Return to Oziach to complete the quest.

Quest Complete!

  • ✅ 20,000 Strength XP
  • ✅ 20,000 Defence XP
  • ✅ Ability to wear Rune Platebody
  • ✅ Ability to wear Green Dragonhide Body
