Client2 Commands Reference

This page documents available developer and cheat commands in the 2004Scape Ported TypeScript Client (Client2).

Account Commands

CommandDescription
::resetReset your progress
::minmeSet all your stats to level 1
::maxmeSet all your stats to level 99
::setstatSet a stat to a specific level
::addxpAdd XP to a skill (data/src/scripts/_test/scripts/cheats/cheat_addxp.rs2)
::setxpSet your XP in a skill
::1hpSet your health to 1
::foodbankFill your bank with food
::energyRestore your run energy

Item Commands

CommandDescription
::bankOpen your bank anywhere
::bank_presetFill your bank with common items
::clearinvClear a named inventory
::giveGive yourself any item
::fmtestGive yourself items to test firemaking
::fmbankFill your bank with firemaking items
::fishtestGive yourself items to test fishing
::giverunesGive yourself items to test magic
::magicbankFill your bank with magic items

Quest Commands

CommandDescription
::~quest / ::~questsQuest commands menu
::~cqComplete all quests
::~rqReset all quests

Teleport Commands

CommandDescription
::teleTeleport to specific coordinates
::tele up / downTeleport up or down a level
::north / ::east / ::south / ::westTeleport 1 tile in a direction
::homeTeleport to Lumbridge
::varrockTeleport to Varrock
::faladorTeleport to Falador
::draynorTeleport to Draynor Village
::portsarimTeleport to Port Sarim
::rimmingtonTeleport to Rimmington
::alkharidTeleport to Al Kharid
::seersTeleport to Seers Village
::giantsTeleport to Giants in Edgeville Dungeon
::brimhavenTeleport to Brimhaven
::entranaTeleport to Entrana

Client and Engine Commands

CommandDescription
::debugToggle the debug overlay
::chatSwitch chat font era
::perfShow FPS
::coord / ::pos / ::zoneGet your position information
::playersGet the number of players online
::busyCheck if your player is busy
::lineofwalk / ::map_blockedVisualize movement or blocked tiles
::delayDelay your player for *n* ticks
::open / ::closeOpen or close interfaces
::seq / ::animPlay a sequence on your player
::spotanimPlay a spot animation on your player
::loc / ::npcSpawn a loc or NPC under your player
